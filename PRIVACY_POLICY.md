# Privacy Policy for MEWallet

**Last updated: December 22, 2025**

## Introduction

MEWallet ("we", "our", or "us") operates the MEWallet mobile application (the "Service"). This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our Service.

## Information We Collect

### Personal Information
- **Name**: For user identification
- **Phone Number**: For authentication and OTP verification
- **Email Address**: For OAuth authentication
- **PIN/Password**: Encrypted for secure authentication
- **Transaction History**: Records of payments and balance operations

### Automatically Collected Information
- **Device Information**: Device model, OS version
- **Usage Data**: App interaction and performance data
- **Log Data**: IP address, access times, app crashes

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected data for:
- **Authentication**: Verify user identity via OTP and OAuth
- **Transaction Processing**: Enable payments between merchants and users
- **Security**: Protect against fraud and unauthorized access
- **Service Improvement**: Enhance app functionality and user experience
- **Notifications**: Send transaction updates and alerts

## Data Storage and Security

- **Database**: Data is stored securely using Supabase with encryption
- **Passwords**: Hashed using bcrypt (never stored in plain text)
- **Transmission**: All data transmitted over HTTPS/WSS
- **Access Control**: Role-based access with JWT tokens

## Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

- **Google OAuth**: For authentication ([Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy))
- **2Factor SMS**: For OTP verification ([2Factor Privacy Policy](https://2factor.in/privacy-policy))
- **Supabase**: For data storage ([Supabase Privacy Policy](https://supabase.com/privacy))

## Data Sharing

We do NOT:
- Sell your personal information
- Share data with advertisers
- Use data for marketing purposes

We MAY share data:
- When required by law
- To protect rights and safety
- With your explicit consent
